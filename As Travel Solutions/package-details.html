🙏 About Ashtavinayak Yatra

🛕 Ashtavinayak (8 Ganpati Temples)

Ashtavinayak refers to the eight sacred temples of Lord Ganesha located around Maharashtra. Completing this yatra is considered highly auspicious and spiritually fulfilling.

🛕 8 Ashtavinayak Temples List

  • 1️⃣ Moreshwar Temple - Location: Morgaon - First and main temple of Ashtavinayak
  • 2️⃣ Siddhivinayak Temple - Location: Siddhatek - Known for wish fulfillment
  • 3️⃣ Ballaleshwar Temple - Location: Pali - Only Ganpati named after devotee
  • 4️⃣ Varadvinayak Temple - Location: Mahad - Known for granting wishes
  • 5️⃣ Chintamani Temple - Location: Theur - Removes worries (Chinta)
  • 6️⃣ Girijatmaj Temple - Location: Lenyadri - Located in caves (unique temple)
  • 7️⃣ Vighnahar Temple - Location: Ozar - Removes obstacles
  • 8️⃣ Mahaganapati Temple - Location: Ranjangaon - Powerful and important Ganpati
